Computer Recycling

Preservation-focused recycling that honors technology's legacy

A Better Approach to E-Waste

At Tri-Force, we believe in preservation over scavenging. While others strip electronics for raw materials, we focus on extending the life of technology and finding new homes for working equipment.

Our approach minimizes environmental harm by reducing the need for destructive material extraction and keeping functional technology out of landfills.

  • Refurbishment and redistribution of working computers
  • Component-level assessment and preservation
  • Data destruction with certificate of sanitization
  • Environmentally responsible handling of non-repairable items
  • Support for vintage and legacy systems

Millions of old phones sit forgotten in drawers. We give them a purpose again.

Your Old Phone Still Has Value

Whether it's a cracked screen, a dead battery, or just an outdated model, your old mobile phone doesn't belong in a landfill. We assess every device for reuse potential before considering material recovery.

Phones contain valuable and hazardous materials alike — from rare earth metals to lithium batteries. Proper handling matters.

  • Full data wipe with certificate of sanitization
  • Battery health assessment and safe disposal
  • Screen and component harvesting for repairs
  • Refurbishment for donation or resale
  • Tablets and smartwatches accepted too
